About

Driven by

At my core, I'm a surfer. That means I'm constantly chasing rhythm, reading environments, and adapting to what's in front of me. That mindset flows into everything I do.

As a photographer and videographer, I focus on capturing authentic moments — the kind that can't be staged or repeated. I'm drawn to natural light, raw emotion, and dynamic environments.

My work as a marketing strategist is rooted in understanding people. I don't just build campaigns — I build narratives that resonate, connect, and convert.

On the technical side, I believe design and functionality should work seamlessly together. I create digital experiences that are visually compelling and performance-driven.
